Human-in-the-Loop AI (HITL) integrates human oversight into AI systems through continuous feedback cycles of data labeling, prediction, human correction, and model refinement. This approach enhances accuracy and fairness, particularly in high-stakes domains like healthcare, finance, and document processing. While HITL offers superior quality control and regulatory compliance, it involves trade-offs in speed and scalability compared to fully automated systems.
